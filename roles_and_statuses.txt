WorkHub Roles and Statuses Guide

1. Organization Member Roles (company_users table, role column)
- 0 = User/Member: Standard team member with access to view and participate in projects/tasks within the organization.
- 1 = Admin: Workspace owner or administrator with permissions to manage settings, invite members, and edit organization resources.

2. Task Statuses (tasks table, status column)
- 1 = To Do: Default status for newly created tasks.
- 2 = In Progress: The task is currently being worked on.
- 3 = Completed: The task is finished.
- 4 = On Hold: The task is temporarily paused.

3. Task Priorities (tasks table, priority column)
- 1 = Low
- 2 = Medium
- 3 = High
- 4 = Urgent

4. Task Types (tasks table, type column)
- 1 = Feature
- 2 = Bug
- 3 = Documentation
- 4 = Research
